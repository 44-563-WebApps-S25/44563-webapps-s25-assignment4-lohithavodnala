Macbeth

Act 1, Scene 1 - A desert place.

Thunder and lightning. Enter three Witches.

First Witch: When shall we three meet again? In thunder, lightning, or in rain?

Second Witch: When the hurlyburly’s done, When the battle’s lost and won.

Third Witch: That will be ere the set of sun.

First Witch: Where the place?

Second Witch: Upon the heath.

Third Witch: There to meet with Macbeth.

First Witch: I come, Graymalkin!

Second Witch: Paddock calls.

Third Witch: Anon.

All: Fair is foul, and foul is fair: Hover through the fog and filthy air.

Exeunt.

Henry IV, Part 2

Act 1, Scene 1 - A tempestuous noise of thunder and lightning heard

[Enter a shipmaster and a boatswain]

Shipmaster

Boatswain, I say!

Boatswain

Here, master: what cheer?

[The shipmaster and boatswain continue their conversation as the ship is in peril]

Shipmaster

Good boatswain, have care. Where's the master? Play the men in the ship. Be the officers: you may appoint the task, and I’ll go and tell the king what to do.

Boatswain

I pray now, keep below.

[Exit]

Shipmaster

Where is the master, boatswain?

Boatswain

Do you not hear him? You mar our labor: keep your cabins: you do assist the storm.

[Exeunt]

Act 1, Scene 2 - London. A street.

[Enter two Keepers]

First Keeper

I know you are a gentleman of good part, and, if I am not mistaken, you have a daughter that loves the gentleman you talk about?

Second Keeper

I am glad to hear that, sir. I will speak with him about it right away.

[Exeunt]

Act 1, Scene 3 - York. The Archbishop's palace.

[Enter the ARCHBISHOP of YORK, the LORDS of YORK, and others]

Archbishop

My lords, I come to tell you of a danger that threatens us. We must act fast, or all will be lost.

Lord

Indeed, we must act swiftly. Our enemies draw near, and we are not prepared!

[The lords discuss their strategy and prepare for battle]

[Exeunt]
